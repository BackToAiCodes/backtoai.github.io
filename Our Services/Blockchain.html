What is Blockchain?

The blockchain, to put it simply, is a distinct distributed record that saves data and ensures its accuracy. Blockchain ensures that transaction continued into the reliable blockchain database by employing a separate set of cryptography-based technologies.

Every monetary transaction is managed by blockchain technology. Blockchain, however, extends to any area where anything of value is transacted, including contracts, personal data, medical records, corporate data, and much more. It is not only confined to the exchange of money.

Today, blockchain has emerged as the most talked-about topic among many businesses, particularly in the IT sector.
